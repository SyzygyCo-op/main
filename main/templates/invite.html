Who are we?

We’re a non-profit focused on building a tightly-knit creative community in San Francisco. We’re driven by the belief that people can accomplish incredible things by working together, and that this culture of collaboration and fellowship is one of the best ways for each of us to grow and thrive.

In this community, we’re here to contribute to each other’s success. We love seeing the people we believe in achieve their goals, reach their potential, and be the best that they can be

Membership

Syzygy is membership based 501(c) nonprofit .

The dues paid by members allows us to have a space to do awesome stuff in and allows us to have money for throwing events and improving the space!

As a member, you receive 24/7 access to the space and co-ownership of all Syzygy resources.

Standard
Membership

$ 150 per month
  • Standard Membership is for people who make more than $70k/year.
  • You help provide funds for us to have our space, host events, and do awesome stuff!

Low Income Membership

$ 50 per month
  • Low Income Membership is available for people making less than $70k/year.
  • This opens up membership for people who make less!
